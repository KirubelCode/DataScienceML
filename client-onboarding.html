Learn More About the Client Onboarding System

Overview

This cloud-based client onboarding system is designed to empower businesses to securely manage and own their customer data. By leveraging APIs and cloud infrastructure, businesses can create seamless onboarding experiences for their clients while maintaining full control over their data.

Key Features

  • Google Account Integration: Send clients a unique link to allow access to specific Google account data, which is securely stored in the business's database.
  • Secure Data Storage: Ensure that all customer data is stored securely on the cloud with best practices for data retrieval and privacy.
  • API Accessibility: Simplify onboarding by enabling API-driven account creation and client information management.
  • Enhanced Decision-Making: Use the stored customer data to develop pricing strategies, predict trends, and improve business operations.

How It Works

The onboarding system operates as a service that integrates into your existing business processes:

  1. A business registers on the platform and sets up their onboarding system.
  2. Clients receive a secure link to share their Google account data.
  3. The system processes and stores client data in the cloud, ensuring secure retrieval for future use.
  4. Businesses utilise the data for analysis, pricing, and decision-making, all through their dedicated portal.

Benefits

  • Scalable cloud-based infrastructure ensures reliable performance even as your business grows.
  • Improves client experience by streamlining the onboarding process.
  • Empowers businesses with actionable data insights to drive better decisions.
  • Highly secure and compliant with data protection regulations.

Technical Details

  • Developed using node.js, sql, mysql, npm.
  • Focus on google api integration, allowing for the seamless retrieval of data.
  • Designed for cloud deployment use for higher accessibility.
